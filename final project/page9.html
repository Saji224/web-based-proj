Ride a camel
A journey through Petra can never be complete without riding on the back of the ship of the desert. Petra’s Bedouins are like any desert people of Jordan; humble, generous and multilingual. Some Bedouins can speak up to seven languages fluently, learned only by engaging with visiting tourists. So, when you ask for a camel ride, they will always be there making sure you are safe and sound.
Marvel At The Sunset View
Get ready to mount 800 stairs and witness the most amazing view of sunset in Petra. The second-most famed monument of Petra, Al-Deir, lets you marvel at the beauty of changing hues of the sky. Make sure you Get mesmerized as the color of the cobblestones turn into rose gold with the last rays of the sun.
Indulge In Shopping
Well, there is no dearth of spots for shopping like Umm Raami’s Shop, Indiana Jones Gifts Shop, Why Not Shop, and the famous Andalusia Bazaar. These shops showcase some of the best natural and traditional crafts like handcrafted jewelry, aromatic soaps and embroidered shawls. Go for a shopping spree while the sun is setting as it is one of the best things to do in Petra at night.
Relax at the Mövenpick Resort Petra
Petra hosts a number of hotels and resorts to suit your taste and budget. Mövenpick Resort Petra is considered the most favoured among tourists for being adjacent to the Al-Khazneh monument and for its stunning authentic interior
Explore the hiking trails.
Wondering what to do in Petra, Jordan? Well, you will be shocked to know that there are umpteen hiking trails, from easy to difficult, that you can opt for. Petra is a city to be explored and not to laze around. So, lace up your boots and go for some hiking trails like the Main Trail, Wadi Farasa Trail. Make sure you keep some snacks and water to replenish yourself and do not forget to pack your camera to capture the beauty that you have not seen before.
Horseback through city ruins.
Discover Petra authentically with a horseback journey, immersing in Jordanian culture by exploring tribes and engaging with locals to hear their stories. This is one of the best things to do in Petra as you will get to experience life above and beyond the trivial matters. So, if you are adventurous soul and looking for a thrilling activity then you must go for a horse ride in Petra.
